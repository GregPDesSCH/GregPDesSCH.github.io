GPD Pong (Only available for PC web browsers)

Built: June - August 2016

A larger version of my small project from UWaterloo's Game Jam Fall 2015 event.
Control Scheme:
Press the up or down arrow keys to move your paddle. On two-player mode, up and down arrow keys controls the paddle on the left, and W and S moves the paddle on the right up and down respectively. Press P to toggle the Pause Menu.
