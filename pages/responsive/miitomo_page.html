Connecting through Miitomo?

Simply have a phone with you with a QR scanner (either built-in or through an application), and scan the QR code you see here. You can also save the image onto your device directly and then use Miitomo to read the image and load it up. (for touch-based phones such as the Nexus 5, just hold your thumb or finger on the phone while visible, then select the option to download and save the photo.)
NOTE: You must have Miitomo installed on the phone to recognize this QR Code, as this is to be read and loaded through Miitomo only.
